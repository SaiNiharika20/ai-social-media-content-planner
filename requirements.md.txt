## Proposed Solution
The **AI-Powered Social Media Content Planner & Optimizer** is a web-based application that uses AI to assist users in generating, planning, and optimizing social media content.

Users provide simple inputs such as:
- Social media platform (Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Business or content category
- Target audience
- Preferred tone (professional, casual, creative)

Based on these inputs, the AI generates:
- Engaging captions
- Relevant hashtags
- Call-to-action (CTA) suggestions
- A weekly social media content calendar
